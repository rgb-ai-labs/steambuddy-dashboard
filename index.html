GST Payable to Government — Quick View
In simple words: we collect GST from customers on every sale, and we pay GST to vendors on every purchase. The GST we paid (called ITC) is allowed to be deducted from the GST we collected — only the leftover has to be paid in cash to the government. Anything still left over carries forward to next month.
